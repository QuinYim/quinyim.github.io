<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">Biography</h2>

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><autocolor>The Hong Kong Polytechnic University</autocolor></a>, working with <a href="https://peng-zhe.github.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><autocolor>Prof. Zhe Peng</autocolor></a>. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Telecommunications Engineering at <a href="https://www.xidian.edu.cn/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><autocolor>Xidian University</autocolor></a>, advised by <a href="https://web.xidian.edu.cn/mawenping/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><autocolor>Prof. Wenping Ma</autocolor></a>. I am interested in resource management as well as security and privacy in future 6G networks. Specifically, my research focuses on communication and network resource management and sharing, as well as blockchain-based network management. I also have a strong passion for security and privacy in distributed networks.
